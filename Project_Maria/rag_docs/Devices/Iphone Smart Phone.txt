3. iPhone Model Naming Conventions
==================================================

Apple uses specific naming patterns to indicate features and position.

- "mini": smaller form factor
- "Plus": larger screen than standard model
- "Pro": advanced features and materials
- "Pro Max": largest size with highest-end features
- "SE": budget-focused model using older design elements

The presence of "Pro" or "Pro Max" typically indicates:
- Higher-performance hardware
- More advanced camera systems
- Premium materials

==================================================

References to models such as:
- "iPhone 16"
- "iPhone 17"
- "iPhone 17 Pro"
- "iPhone 17 Pro Max"

are commonly used by the public to describe *expected future generations* based on
Apple’s historical naming pattern.

However:

- Specifications, names, and variants may change before release.

Therefore, future models should be discussed only in general terms unless officially announced.
