3. Samsung Galaxy Naming Conventions
==================================================

Samsung model names indicate position and features.

- "S": Premium flagship series
- "Ultra": Highest-end variant in a generation
- "+": Larger size than base model
- "Note": Productivity-focused with stylus
- "Z": Foldable design
- "A": Mid-range / budget
- "M": Battery-focused budget models

Higher numbers generally indicate newer generations.
