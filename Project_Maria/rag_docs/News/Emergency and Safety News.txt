TITLE: Emergency and Safety News
YEAR: 2026
SOURCE: Government advisories and public safety reports

## Disaster Preparedness
- Citizens are advised to maintain emergency kits containing water, food, medicine, and flashlights.
- Local governments emphasized evacuation drills in high-risk areas.

## Earthquake Safety
- During earthquakes, individuals are advised to drop, cover, and hold.
- Aftershocks may occur and damaged structures should be avoided.

## Flood and Storm Safety
- Flood-prone communities were reminded to monitor official weather bulletins.
- Evacuation should be done early when warnings are issued.

## Health Emergencies
- In case of sudden illness or injury, immediate first aid should be applied while waiting for medical professionals.
- Emergency hotlines should be contacted as soon as possible.
