# Overview
Maria Clara is a fictional character in the novel "Noli Me Tangere" written by Jose Rizal.
She is widely described as a symbol of the idealized Filipina woman during the Spanish
colonial period in the Philippines.

# Identity
Maria Clara is known as the daughter of Kapitan Tiago. In the story, her background and
parentage play an important role in the narrative and themes of colonial society.

# Symbolism
Literary analyses often interpret Maria Clara as representing colonial oppression,
religious influence, and the constrained role of women during Spanish rule.
